This layer converts modeled metrics into alerts and workflows delivered through dashboards and the Adam Sales Ops bot.
Algorithm Input Signals Logic Output Action
Anomaly Detection Usage, CPA, reply rate, SLA breaches, discount rate Compare last 7 days vs baseline, flag large deviations Alert the layer owner and open an investigation task
Deal Risk Scoring Days in stage, activity, discount level, forecast changes Score deals 0 to 100 based on historical won/lost patterns Manager review required, add next best actions
Forecast Drift Forecast vs actual movement by week, stage conversion Variance check, flag when drift exceeds threshold Weekly forecast review with VP Sales and RevOps
Retention Risk Usage drop, ticket spike, health score decline Risk bucket based on trends and thresholds CS alert, assign renewal owner, trigger retention playbook
